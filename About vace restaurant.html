Our Story

Dalla nostra famiglia al vostro — Since 1976, the Calcagno family has crafted authentic Italian flavors for our community.

Milestones

1976

Our Store was founded by Blanca & Valerio Calcagn They launched its flagship location in Washington, DC. From the outset, it earned a reputation as the epitome of an Italian delicatessen. Nearly four decades later, Vace remains committed to providing traditional deli services with a touch of "old country charm" in a modernized era where personalized service is increasingly rare.

2000s

Offering over 60 in-house crafted items, including freshly baked pizza and delectable homemade pasta, our goal is to uphold the tradition of wholesome, all-natural products at affordable prices. The Calcagno Family expresses heartfelt gratitude to our customers for their unwavering support of our small, family-owned, and operated local business.
